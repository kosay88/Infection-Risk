How does it work?


Using an estimate of the quanta generation rate, this app helps you calculate the development of quanta concentration over time for an indoor space with given dimensions and ventilation rate. The risk of infection is calculated by integrating the quanta generation rate in the Wells-Riley equation.

Feel free to specify room dimensions and a representative ventilation rate in above boxes to give it a try. Don't know the ventilation rate? Well, 3/hour can be a good first guess.
